# Loudoun County Landfill Research Notes

## Scope
- Republic Services Loudoun County Solid Waste landfill on Highway 72
- 7-acre expansion (19 more acres available)
- Community goals: stop expansion, force real recycling, ban PFAS/medical/radioactive waste

## Research Targets
1. Contract between Loudoun County Solid Waste Commission and Republic Services
2. TDEC permitting status for 7-acre expansion
3. Public records on waste acceptance (PFAS, medical, radioactive)
4. Meeting minutes from Loudoun County Solid Waste Commission
5. Precedent cases: Murfreesboro and Scott County, TN landfill expansion stops

## Search Strategy
- TDEC databases and permit search
- Loudoun County government records
- News articles (local TN news, environmental orgs)
- FOIA/public records requests
- Court records for precedent cases
