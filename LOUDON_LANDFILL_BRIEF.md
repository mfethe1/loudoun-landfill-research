# Loudon County Landfill Situation Brief
## Republic Services / Matlock Bend Landfill

**Prepared:** May 23, 2026
**For:** Chuck Fethe & Community Organizing Team

---

## The Problem

Republic Services operates the **Matlock Bend Landfill** on Highway 72 in Loudon County, TN. They are the operator; Loudon County Solid Waste Disposal Commission (LCSWDC) owns the property but has limited control under a poorly written contract.

### Key Issues

- **Expansion underway:** 7 acres currently in TDEC permitting, with 19 additional acres available
- **Zero real recycling:** Loudon County recycles nothing except some cardboard. Republic charges recycling fees but mixes it all with regular garbage
- **Problematic waste accepted with no reporting:**
  - PFAS (not yet banned)
  - Medical waste (often contains Teflon)
  - Commercial nuclear/radioactive waste from hospitals within 75-mile radius
  - No statistics or limits on radioactive waste intake
- **Importing waste from 75 miles away:** Other counties selectively ship their worst waste to Loudon County
- **State mandate ignored:** Tennessee requires all counties to reduce landfill inflow and increase recycling
- **Community engagement collapsing:** Strong initial turnout at rezoning meetings, now only 5-6 people attending
